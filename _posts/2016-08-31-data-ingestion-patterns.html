Data acquisition is one of the most crucial aspects of the data ecosystem and can turn painful if not appropriately dealt with. Therefore, I wanted to outline some of the standard ingestion patterns in play and provide visibility:

  1. Batch Ingestion pattern - where the data is batched and ingested
  2. Lambda Ingestion pattern - where the data is considered both as a batch and a stream and handled separately by batch and speed layers and served together by providing views on each of the layers.
  3. Kappa Ingestion pattern - where the data is considered as a stream and ingested as a stream.
I will go into details with examples in subsequent posts.
