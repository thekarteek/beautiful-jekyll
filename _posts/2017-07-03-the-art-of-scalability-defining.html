This is a continuation of my previous post - https://goo.gl/63N6Wg on attempting to define microservices.

As I mentioned in earlier post, the monolithic way of scaling is helpful but not quite. It could be further strengthened by designing the application as a collection of microservices that talk to each other (and that perform each individual business function) as individual applications do as opposed to a monolithic application. Thus functionally decomposing the application into multiple mini-applications (services).

Now, each particular application (services) can be scaled along X-axis and Z-axis again for scalability and availability. Scalability and availability make more sense when you apply it after decomposing it into multiple applications.
